Hi, my name is Steven (Shang-Kuan) Lin

I recently completed my PhD in Genomic Medicine and Statistics, where I gained expertise in analysing large datasets and developing novel computational methods to understand infectious diseases better. My research focused on investigating infectious diseases from both the perspective of the human host and the pathogen, as well as exploring the interactions between the two. As I continue to explore the intersection of technology and medicine, I am excited to expand my expertise into the field of AI/ML and their application to computational biology. I am also proficient in programming languages such as R, Python, and Bash scripting, which have been essential tools in my research. Currently, I am seeking new opportunities to expand my expertise in either an academic or industry setting. I am eager to explore new challenges and collaborate with like-minded professionals who share my passion for using computational approaches to advance our understanding of biology and medicine. Through this website, I aim to showcase my skills and experience in computational biology to potential employers and collaborators. Whether you're interested in learning more about my research, exploring my portfolio of projects, or discussing potential industry/academia positions, I am always open to new opportunities and connections. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
